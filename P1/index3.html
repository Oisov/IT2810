In 2006 Bouygues Telecom and Vodafone decided to merge. As this was a controversial merger between the biggest internet provider in respectively Germany and France, they decided to renew with a new company name and logo. The company logo was choosen through a competition between 12 renowned artists, 6 from Germany and 6 from France. Urszula Donovan won with the contribution you see below.
